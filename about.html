About Us

Industry Is A Better Horse To Ride Than Genius

Since the year 2005 Indian govt started focusing on the development of infrastructure may it be roads, bridges, buildings, dams and others and hence, demand for the raw materials was to increase tremendously.

Considering this as one of the motivating factor and to meet the growing need of cement, promoters started building a company which they named as “AADI CEMENT PVT LTD”. The company became functional in the year 2008.

Since then, company has passed through many hurdles and then also made a successful growth story in terms of brand establishment, physical presence and finance.

In the year 2014, SUMERU TRADELINK PVT LTD took over 100% stakes of this company and started operating the company.

Since 2014, company has come up with various new products, marketing strategies and achieved various accreditations for the manufacturing plant.

These all collectively have put the company on the next level.Current promoters of the company are highly qualified, experienced in the relevant field and quite professional.They understand the market well and always try to match the expectations of their associates and customers.

It has been unanimously accepted amongst the small scale industries that the quality and services provided by the company is excellent. Management is time to time upgrading the technology to produce the cement at par with the international standards.

Company is built over the 6 acre land on the main road of SH 158 between Idar and Vadali. Company has got their own lime stone mine, which is 30 km away from the factory.Other assets in the form of machinery includes three kilns, crusher, loader, two silos and three grinding mills etc.Company is capable of producing 1 lac MT cement per annum.Special efforts are taken to ensure that the plant upholds the latest environmental norms and remains clean and dust free

Our Mission
  • Double sales and profit in coming years
  • Achieve operational excellence
  • Create superior value for customer through premium products & brand positioning

Our Vision
  • To be a profitably growing and caring company, to become a significant player in operating market
